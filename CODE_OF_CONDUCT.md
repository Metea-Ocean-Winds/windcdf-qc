# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and professional
environment for everyone contributing to WindCDF, regardless of background,
experience level, or affiliation.

We value constructive collaboration, technical rigor, and open discussion.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in language and actions
- Provide constructive feedback and technical arguments
- Be open to different viewpoints and experiences
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Assume good intent from others

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or dismissive comments
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a technical setting

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards of acceptable
behavior and may take appropriate corrective action in response to violations.

This includes warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from the project
community if necessary.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces, including:
- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Discussions and reviews
- Any other communication channels related to WindCDF
